# 📥 Raw Information Ingestion | `raw/`

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The workspace's unstructured inbox. Users can dump raw documents, chat transcripts, or brain dumps here for agents to process.

---

## ⚠️ Process Lifecycle
1. **Extract & Structure:** Analyze the context of raw files to determine their correct target in `docs/` or `roadmap/`.
2. **Translate to Spec:** Clean up, format, and structure the data.
3. **Clean Inbox:** Save the structured file in its target location, then delete the raw input source to keep this inbox empty.
