# 📂 Replaced & Legacy Archive | `archive/`

---

## 🎯 Purpose
This directory acts as the historical vault of the workspace. **Never delete substantial legacy code or specs permanently**; instead, relocate them here.

---

## ⚠️ Operational Rules
1. **Preserve History:** Move outdated milestones, deprecated features, and retired logic to this folder.
2. **Context Retention:** Prepend a warning or timestamp to the top of any archived document explaining why and when it was archived.
